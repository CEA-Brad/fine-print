How it works

Four steps. Zero legal expertise required.

1

Visit any TOS page

Fine Print automatically detects Terms of Service, Privacy Policies, and similar legal pages.

2

AI reads it for you

The page text is sent to your AI provider (Claude or GPT) for analysis. You bring your own API key.

3

See what matters

Concerning clauses are highlighted on the page. A floating badge shows the privacy score.

4

Protect yourself

Get actionable recommendations for privacy tools based on the specific concerns found.

Features

⚙

Auto-detection

Automatically identifies TOS, privacy policies, cookie policies, EULAs, and more.

★

Privacy scoring

Every document gets a 1-10 score so you can compare services at a glance.

⚠

Inline highlights

Concerning clauses are color-coded directly on the page — red, yellow, and blue by severity.

📄

Plain-English summary

Key points explain what the policy says in normal language, not legalese.

🔒

BYOK model

Bring your own API key. Your data goes directly to your AI provider — no middleman.

🛠

Tool recommendations

Get suggestions for VPNs, ad blockers, and privacy tools based on specific concerns.
