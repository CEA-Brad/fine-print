How it works

  1. Fine Print detects when you visit a Terms of Service or Privacy Policy page.
  2. The page text is sent to your AI provider for analysis.
  3. Concerning clauses are highlighted directly on the page.
  4. A summary with recommendations appears in the side panel.

Your API key is stored only in your browser's local storage and is sent only to the API provider you select. Fine Print has no backend server.
